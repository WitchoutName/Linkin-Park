With You




Lyrics

With You is the third track on Linkin Park's debut album Hybrid Theory. It starts with a sample and consists
of Mike Shinoda rapping, with Chester Bennington singing the chorus with added raps from Shinoda. The last
performance of the song was in the Summer Sonic in 2006. That show was the second to last performance of
Runaway
